You Are Not in One Bucket

A business grows. Adds a service. Hires a crew. Takes on a subcontractor. The policy? Still sitting there like it's 2019. Unbothered. Unchanged.

Here's what carriers won't tell you: insurance underwriters classify your operations using NAICS codes — and if you've grown, you're likely touching 4, 5, maybe 6 different codes without knowing it.

If it's not named in your policy — it's not covered. Yes, even that thing you've been doing for two years.

Preventative Maintenance — For Your Coverage

Most businesses don't find out until it's too late. Here's when the gap reveals itself:

  • An injury happens on a job type added two years ago
  • A client loss ties back to a service you just started offering
  • A vehicle incident involves a role your policy never anticipated

That's the moment "we thought we were covered" becomes the most expensive sentence in business.
